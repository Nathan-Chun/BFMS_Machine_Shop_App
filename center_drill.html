Center Drill Speed and Feed Calculator

Tool Information

Center drills are used to create a lead in hole that helps align drills and should always precede drill toolpaths. They are typically made of carbide, which is a much harder material than high speed steel (HSS) used in drills.

- retract is high enough to avoid collision with part
