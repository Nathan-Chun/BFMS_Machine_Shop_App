Center Drill Speed and Feed Calculator

Tool Information

Center drills are used to create a lead in hole that helps align drills and should always precede drill toolpaths. They are typically made of Carbide or High Speed Steel (HSS). This calculator will provide feeds and speeds for HSS center drill bits.

Programming Checklist:
- drill depths < cutting length denoted as LF in the figure below
- retract is high enough to avoid collision with part
- RPM ≤ 12000
